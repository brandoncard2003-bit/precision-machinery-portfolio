# Metal Model Rocket

[← Back to Portfolio](../README.md)

**Type:** Machining
**Tools:** HAAS CNC Lathe
**Tolerance:** 0.005 inches
**Material:** Aluminum

---

## Overview

A 10-piece scale model rocket machined entirely from aluminum on a HAAS CNC 
Lathe. Each component was individually machined, then tapped and threaded for 
assembly. Completed over approximately 4 weeks in the lab.
