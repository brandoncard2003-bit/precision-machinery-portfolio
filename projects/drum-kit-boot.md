# Drum Kit Boot

[← Back to Portfolio](../README.md)

**Type:** 3D Printing | Original Design
**Tools:** Tinkercad, Creality Ender 3
**Material:** Ataraxia Art Black FPLA Filament

---

## Overview
Designed and printed a replacement boot for a drum kit from scratch after 
finding no suitable off-the-shelf replacement. Created original blueprints 
and tolerances independently. Iterated through a failed first prototype 
by identifying and correcting measurement errors in the inside diameter, 
outside diameter, and length. Final print completed in 9 hours 51 minutes 
on a Creality Ender 3.
