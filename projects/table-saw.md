**Tools:** HAAS Manual Lathe, HAAS Manual Mill, HAAS CNC Mill
**Tolerance:** 0.0005 inches
**Material:** Aluminum, Cold-Rolled Steel

---

## Overview

A 13-piece desktop table saw built from aluminum and cold-rolled steel. 
Required sourcing carbide milling tools and calculating precise RPM and feed 
rates for cutting steel. CNC G-Code was written and executed on a HAAS CNC 
mill for select components. Tolerances as tight as 0.0005 inches were 
achieved throughout the build.
